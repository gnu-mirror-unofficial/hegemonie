Introduction

GNU Hégémonie is a multiplayer 3D game featuring a gameplay inspired by XPilot, but in a maritime world.

Downloading

We have not released any official version yet.

Mailing List

At the moment, there is only one mailing list for GNU Hégémonie hegemonie-devel@gnu.org [subscribe] [informations] [archives], dedicated to its development.
We will open a user mailing-list as soon as we are closer from an official release.

Development

You can follow the development of Hégémonie through our mailing list, and go to our Savannah project page
